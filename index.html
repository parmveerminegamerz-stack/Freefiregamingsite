🎯 One-Tap Drag Headshot Trick

Free Fire me perfect headshot lagane ke liye is technique ka istemal karein:

  • White Aim: Apna aim hamesha enemy ke shareer ke thoda paas rakhein taaki wo red ho jaye, par dhyan rahe ki wo pair par lock na ho.
  • Straight Drag: Agar enemy ek jagah khada hai, toh apne fire button ko seedhe upar ki taraf khinchein.
  • Rotation Drag: Agar enemy right ya left bhaag raha hai, toh fire button ko uski taraf ghumate hue upar khinchein.
